The STEP UP Outreach Education Program aims to

STEP UP Academic Research Program

Think of the STEP UP Academic Research Program as
a laboratory committed to identifying ‘what works’ in
education through an innovative, rigorous and
interdisciplinary approach.

Why should we care? Because education drives vital
life-long outcomes such as health, income, poverty,
parenting, social isolation, equity, and eliminating
educational gaps makes for a more just and thriving
society for all.
